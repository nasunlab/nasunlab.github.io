Join Our Lab

We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team. Below are some of the opportunities available to join our lab.

Opportunities for Bioinformaticians

If you have expertise in bioinformatics and are passionate about analyzing large-scale single-cell and spatial datasets, we invite you to apply for a position in our lab. We are particularly interested in candidates with experience in machine learning, deep learning, and the integration of multi-omics data. Please apply through the Whitehead Institute Careers page. Please also send your CV, a brief statement of research interests, and contact information for three references to nasun@wi.mit.edu.

Opportunities for Data Scientists

Our lab is looking for skilled data scientists who can develop computational models to understand cell-cell communication and disease mechanisms. Candidates should have a strong background in algorithm development, deep learning and programming. To apply, please send your CV and a brief description of your data science experience and research goals to nasun@wi.mit.edu.

Opportunities for Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome applications from postdoctoral candidates interested in our research areas. To apply, please send your CV, a 2-page research statement outlining your past work and future research goals, and contact information for three references.

Opportunities for Visiting Students

We welcome applications from visiting students interested in conducting research in bioinformatics, computational biology, AI for biomedical research or related fields. Please send your CV, a short research proposal, and your availability for a visit to nasun@wi.mit.edu.

Potential Collaborators

We are always open to collaboration with other labs and researchers. If you have an idea for a joint project or would like to discuss potential collaboration opportunities, please reach out to us at nasun@wi.mit.edu.
